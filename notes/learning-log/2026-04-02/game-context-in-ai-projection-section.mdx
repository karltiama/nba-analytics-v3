## Context

Teammate injury split data lived under the Injuries section; product direction is to surface it as **game context** next to the AI Projection Summary until a real model ships.

## Goal

Move **Game context** (injury-based teammate PTS splits) into the **AI Projection Summary** card, label it clearly, and keep the Injuries section for the raw report only.

## Files Changed

- `components/betting/MatchupPageLayout.tsx` — relocate UI; footer copy “Full AI projection — coming soon”.

## Implementation Notes (key logic explained)

- Data source unchanged: still `injuryMatchupContext` from `GET /api/betting/games/[gameId]/details`.
- **Game context** is a sub-block under summary chips, separated by a top border; uppercase micro-label + short disclaimer.
- Removed duplicate block from **Injuries** to avoid two homes for the same tables.

## Key Decisions (and alternatives considered)

- **Single placement:** One surface for splits avoids scroll duplication; Injuries stays authoritative for listings.
- **“Full AI projection” wording:** Distinguishes future model output from static context.

## Assumptions

- Users who rely on the nav pill “AI Projection” will find context there first.

## Failure Modes
